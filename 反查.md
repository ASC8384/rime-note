## 旧方式-reverse_lookup_translator
```
schema/dependencies:
  - terra_pinyin.extended #会生成".reverse.bin"文件

recognizer/patterns/reverse_lookup: "^'[a-z]*$"

translators/reverse_lookup_translator

reverse_lookup:
  dictionary: terra_pinyin_extended
  prefix: "'" #和patterns定义的符号一致
  tips: 【拼音反查】
  preedit_format:
    - xform/([nl])v/$1ü/
    - xform/([nl])ue/$1üe/
    - xform/([jqxy])v/$1u/

```
